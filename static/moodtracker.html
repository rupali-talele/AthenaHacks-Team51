Your Mood History

It's important to recognize how you've been feeling. Mood can changes from day to day and it's totally normal! It is great if you feel positive overall, but it is also okay if you feel negative. Feel free to reach out to friends and family, and the social workers who will listen and care for you.

Here is a mood tracker that shows how you feel in the past month. From the graph, can you tell are you feeling overall positive or negative?
We take a positive emotion as a +1 score, and a negative emotion as a -1 score. For each day, we sum up your responses in your daily reflection. For example. you answer, "happy"(+1), "angry"(-1) and "focus"(+1), so your score will be sum to +1

We also gather all of the emotions you told me in your daily reflection for the past month, and display it in a word cloud. The bigger the text, the more often you were feeling in that way. From the graph, can you tell what are your most common emotions?
